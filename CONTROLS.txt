For playing with keyboard:
				WASD - Movement
				Space - Jump
				Left Click - Attack
				E - Lock Camera
				Q - Toggle lock
				Mouse movement - Look with the camera

For playing with controller (recommended):
				
				Left stick - Movement
				X / A button - Jump
				Circle / B button - Attack
				R1 / RB - Lock camera
				L1 / LB - Toggle lock
				Right stick - Look with the camera
